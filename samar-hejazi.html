Samar Hejazi

We visit Samar Hejazi in her studio with the Akin Collective and furtively explore the industrial basement of the Museum of Contemporary Art in Toronto.

About the artist

Samar Hejazi is a Palestinian artist working and living in Toronto, Canada. She explores her heritage and observes the complex conversations between social constructivism, ethnology and the ethos of modernity by using different craft practices. This results in a body of work consisting of different materials, forms and techniques. Follow Samar on Instagram to see more of her delicate and powerful work.
